Yes 👍 **You need to start ngrok only if your webhook endpoint is running on your local machine.**

Clerk (or Stripe) servers must be able to **reach your API URL from the internet**. Your **localhost is not accessible publicly**, so **ngrok creates a public URL** that forwards to your local server.
